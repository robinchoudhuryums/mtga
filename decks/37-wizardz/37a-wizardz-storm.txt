#: name: Wizardz — Value/Storm (copy + draw-two)
#: format: Standard
#: colors: UBR
#: tier: B — a grindier, value-leaning cousin of the aggressive base (37). Same Grixis Wizard
#: tier: spellslinger spine, but tilted toward card advantage and spell-copy payoffs: draw a
#: tier: pile (Phyrexian Arena / Super Intelligence / cantrips), turn "draw your second card"
#: tier: into board (Erudite Wizard, Clinquant Skymage, Construct a Cosmic Cube), and blow
#: tier: out with copiers (Thousand-Year Storm, Kitsa, Taigam) on a high-value spell. Floor
#: tier: is B-to-A on card advantage + interaction; held at B by singleton variance and the
#: tier: copy-payoff caveat below (copies don't fire the pings). A distinct build to compare.
#: based-on: deck.txt
#: archetype: Grixis (U-primary) Wizard VALUE/STORM. The base 37 races with ping triggers; this
#: archetype: variant grinds: heavy card draw feeds "draw your second card" payoffs and fuels a
#: archetype: spell chain that copiers (Thousand-Year Storm scaling per spell, Kitsa/Taigam per
#: archetype: turn) turn into blowouts. Niv-Mizzet + the pingers are still the kill.
#: notes: RULES CAVEAT that shapes this build: a COPY is put on the stack, not CAST — so copies
#: notes: do NOT trigger "whenever you cast a noncreature spell" (Coruscation Mage / Black Waltz
#: notes: / the 0/1 tokens) or prowess. Copiers here DOUBLE THE EFFECT (removal, burn, draw),
#: notes: they don't storm the pings. Taigam is the exception that helps the ping plan: it
#: notes: suspends the original spell back, giving a real future CAST. So this build leans on
#: notes: copy-for-VALUE + the draw-two engine, not a ping-storm.
#: notes: vs base 37: +Thousand-Year Storm, +Kitsa (craft), +Taigam, +Return the Favor (copiers);
#: notes: +Super Intelligence, +Construct a Cosmic Cube, +Clinquant Skymage (craft) (draw-two);
#: notes: -the most aggressive one-of bodies (Razzle-Dazzler, Harbinger) and a little cheap burn.
#: notes: 25 lands for the heavier curve. Copies are fungible, so the shared single-copy duals
#: notes: overlap with base 37 at no cost. WIP — several craft targets (Kitsa, Clinquant, etc.).
#: protect: Coruscation Mage; Niv-Mizzet, Visionary; Thousand-Year Storm; Taigam, Master Opportunist; Kefka, Court Mage
